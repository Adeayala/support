## Common Issues
### 1. Pieces OS Crashes and Integration Issues
Users report Pieces OS crashes, particularly on Windows, often linked to native_extensions_plugin.dll.  Troubleshooting includes reinstalling, disabling antivirus/firewall, and updating drivers.  Additionally, users experience difficulty downloading local LLMs on Linux and linking multiple cloud accounts (Google, GitHub) simultaneously across different OS versions.

### 2. Pieces Desktop App Issues with Local LLM Downloads and Account Linking
Users are experiencing difficulties with the Pieces Desktop app, including issues with local LLM downloads getting stuck during initialization on Linux and Windows, and persistent problems with linking multiple accounts (Google and GitHub) simultaneously.  Workarounds such as updating Pieces OS or disabling GitHub email privacy have not resolved these issues.  Users have reported these problems for an extended period, and support interactions, including data resets, have not provided a permanent solution.
