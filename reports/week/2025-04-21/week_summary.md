## Common Issues
### 1. Pieces OS Crashes and Integration Issues
Users report Pieces OS crashes due to native_extensions_plugin.dll on Windows and difficulties downloading local LLMs on Linux.  Additionally, some users experience issues simultaneously linking Google and GitHub accounts, with only one account remaining linked at a time. Troubleshooting includes reinstalling the program, disabling plugins, checking firewall/antivirus, and updating graphics drivers.  Support has been contacted regarding the account linking issue, but a resolution has not yet been found.

### 2. Pieces Desktop App Issues with Local LLM Downloads and Account Linking
Users are experiencing difficulties with the Pieces Desktop app, including issues with local LLM downloads getting stuck during initialization on Linux and Windows, and persistent problems with linking multiple accounts (Google and GitHub) simultaneously.  Workarounds such as updating Pieces OS or disabling GitHub email privacy have not resolved these issues.  Users have reported these issues over an extended period, with some dating back several years.
