## Common Issues
### 1. Pieces OS Crashes and Integration Issues
Users report Pieces OS crashes, particularly on Windows, often linked to the native_extensions_plugin.dll.  Suggested workarounds include reinstalling the program, disabling antivirus/firewall, and updating graphics drivers.  Additionally, users experience difficulties downloading local LLMs on Linux and linking multiple cloud accounts (Google and GitHub) simultaneously across different OS versions.

### 2. Pieces Desktop App Issues with Local LLMs and Account Linking
Users report persistent issues with the Pieces Desktop app, including difficulties linking multiple accounts (Google and GitHub) simultaneously and problems downloading or canceling local LLM downloads.  Account linking issues involve one account being unlinked when another is added, despite troubleshooting and support interaction.  Local LLM download problems manifest as downloads getting stuck on initializing or 0%, affecting Windows and Linux users.  Some users have attempted workarounds like disabling email privacy settings or updating the app, but the issues persist.
